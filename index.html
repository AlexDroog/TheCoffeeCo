A Little About Us

We began with a purpose. To bring beautifully crafted coffee to Torontonians and soon the world. With our quality coffee grinded same day in house, our customers will recieve the high quality coffee we are known so well for. When we began in 2011, we were a small startup, funded soley by our creators and staff who felt that we were all really onto something. Fast forward 10 years and we have expanded to 9 locations across the Greater Toronto Area with plans to expand father, even internationally. With every cup, you help us get closer to that goal and we thank you for that. So cheers to another coffee, and we hope to see you inside soon.
